Overtaking (162-169)(0001).

162, (0002).

Before overtaking, you should make sure(0003).

·         the road is sufficiently clear ahead(0004).

·         road users are not beginning to overtake you(0005).

·         there is a suitable gap, in front of the road user, you plan to overtake(0006).

163, (0007).

 

b0002

Overtake only when it is safe, and legal to do so(0008). You should(0009).

·         not get too close, to the vehicle you intend to overtake(0010).

·         use your mirrors, signal when it is safe to do so, take a quick sideways glance, if necessary, into the blind spot area, and then start to move out(0011).

·         not assume that you can simply follow a vehicle ahead, which is also overtaking; there may only be enough room for one vehicle(0012).

·         move quickly past the vehicle you are overtaking, once you have started to overtake(0013). Allow plenty of room(0014). Move back to the left, as soon as you can, but do not cut in(0015).

·         take extra care at night, and in poor visibility, when it is harder to judge, speed, and distance(0016).

·         give way to oncoming vehicles, before passing parked vehicles, or other obstructions, on your side of the road(0017).

 

b0003

·         only overtake on the left, if the vehicle in front is signalling to turn right, and there is room to do so(0018).

·         stay in your lane, if traffic is moving slowly in queues(0019). If the queue on your right, is moving more slowly than you are, you may pass on the left(0020).

·         give motor-cyclists, cyclists, and horse riders, at least as much room as you would, when overtaking a car (see drawing, and Rules 211-215)(0021).

Remember: Mirrors - Signal - Manoeuvre(0022).
