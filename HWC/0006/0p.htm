At traffic lights, 21(0001).

There may be special signals for pedestrians(0002). You should only start to cross, the road, when the green figure shows(0003). If you have started to cross the road, and the green figure goes out, you should still have time to reach the other side, but do not delay(0004). If no pedestrian signals have been provided, watch carefully, and do not cross until the traffic lights are red, and the traffic has stopped(0005). Keep looking, and check for traffic, that may be turning the corner(0006). Remember that traffic lights, may let traffic move in some lanes, while traffic in other lanes, has stopped(0007).

 

Pelican crossings, 22(0008).

These are signal-controlled crossings operated by pedestrians(0009). Push the control button to activate the traffic signals(0010). When the red figure shows, do not cross(0011). When a steady green figure shows, check the traffic has stopped then cross with care(0012). When the green figure begins to flash you should not start to cross _see drawing)(0013). If you have already started you should have time to finish crossing safely(0014).

Puffin crossings, 23(0015).

They differ from pelican crossings, as the red, and green figures, are above the control box, on your side of the road, and there is no flashing green figure phase(0016). Press the button, and wait for the green figure to show(0017).

24, (0018).

When the road is congested, traffic on your side of the road, may be forced to stop, even though their lights are green(0019). Traffic may still be moving on the other side of the road, so press the button, and wait for the signal to cross(0020).
