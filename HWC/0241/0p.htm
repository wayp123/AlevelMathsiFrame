On the kerb, or at the edge of the carriageway(0001).

Loading restrictions on roads, other than Red Routes(0002).

 

Yellow marks on the kerb, or at the edge of the carriageway, indicate that loading, or unloading is prohibited at the times shown, on the nearby black, and white plates(0003). You may stop while passengers board, or alight(0004). If no days are indicated on the signs, the restrictions are in force every day including Sundays, and Bank Holidays(0005). ALWAYS CHECK THE TIMES SHOWN ON THE PLATES(0006). Lengths of road reserved for vehicles loading, and unloading, are indicated by a white bay marking, with the words, Loading Only, and a sign with the white on blue, trolley symbol(0007). This sign also shows whether loading, and unloading, is restricted to goods vehicles, and the times at which the bay can be used(0008). If no times, or days are shown, it may be used at any time(0009). Vehicles may not park here, if they are not loading, or unloading(0010).
