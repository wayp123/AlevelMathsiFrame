Seat belts, and child restraints, 99(0001).

You MUST wear a seat belt in cars, vans, and other goods vehicles, if one is fitted {see table}(0002). Adults, and children aged 14 years, and over, MUST use a seat belt, or child restraint, where fitted, when seated in minibuses, buses, and coaches(0003). Exemptions are allowed for the holders of medical exemption certificates, and those making deliveries, or collections in goods vehicles, when travelling less than 50 metres {approx 162 feet}(0004).
