Crossings_(0001). (9980).

At all crossings, 18(0002).

When using any type of crossing you should(0003).

·       always check that the traffic has stopped, before you start to cross, or push a pram onto a crossing(0004).

·       always cross between the studs, or over the zebra markings(0005). Do not cross at the side of the crossing, or on the zig-zag lines, as it can be dangerous(0006).

You MUST NOT loiter on any type of crossing(0007).

Law (0008).

b0002

 

Zebra crossings, 19(0009).

Give traffic plenty of time to see you, and to stop before you start to cross(0010). Vehicles will need more time when the road is slippery(0011). Wait until traffic has stopped from both directions, or the road is clear before crossing(0012). Remember that traffic does not have to stop, until someone has moved onto the crossing(0013). Keep looking both ways, and listening, in case a driver, or rider has not seen you, and attempts to overtake a vehicle that has stopped(0014).
