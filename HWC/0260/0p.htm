The Defensive Driving System(0001).

 

Defensive driving elements(0002).

There is a system of driving defensively, which is intended to reduce the risk of having a collision(0003). The purpose is to maintain a clear space around your vehicle at all times(0004). This is a very important aspect of safe driving(0005).

 

The Basic Elements are:(0006).

• SEARCH and look in all directions for potential hazards(0007).

• IDENTIFY the type of hazard, if any(0008).

• PREDICT and evaluate the possible dangers related to the threat(0009).

• DECIDE what action to take: steer, reduce speed or stop (0010).

 

The most common Hazards are:(0040).

Static Hazards: Side Junctions, Traffic lights, Pedestrian crossings, Ice(0041).

Moving Hazards: Cars, Bikes, Cycles, Pedestrians, Animals(0042).

Defensive driving procedure(0011).

The basic procedure, when carrying out a lane change manoeuvre is, as follows:(0012).

 

1, OBSERVE(0013). Look in the mirrors to see if it is safe behind you before signalling your intentions(0014).

2, BLIND SPOT(0015). Look over your shoulder, turning your head, to check behind you where the mirrors dont show the full situation(0016).

3, SIGNAL(0017). If it is safe, signal your intension, using indicators or brake lights as appropriate(0018).

4, OBSERVE(0019). Check again, that it is still safe, behind and alongside you, before making the change(0020).

5, BRAKES(0021). Use the brakes to reduce speed if necessary(0022).

6, GEARS(0023). Select the correct gear if necessary(0024).

7, BLIND SPOT(0025). Check the blind spot again, if moving to the left, or right(0026).

8, STEER(0027). If it is still safe, turn in the direction indicated(0028).

9, ACCELERATE(0029). Increase speed as necessary(0030). (drive at the speed of the traffic and conditions, not too slow or faster than the speed limit(0031).

10, INDICATORS(0032). Ensure that the direction indicators are cancelled after completing the manoeuvre(0033). If turning off the road, do not indicate too early, there may be other side roads before the road you want to turn down(0034).

Note(0035). If you intend to return to your original lane, repeat the above procedure(0036). If the traffic in the lane you want to go to is moving faster than you are, slow down to allow a gap to build up in front of you, to give yourself a runway, to increase your speed, to match the other lane(0037). You must have a sufficient gap to slow down, in-case the traffic in front of you slows down, or you can't find a gap, in the lane you want to go to(0038).

 

If your parked view is blocked, by a large vehicle, you may need to get out of your vehicle, to see(0050). At night, in bright conditions, you can turn on your interior light, to act as sunglasses(0051). Use Aircon as a De-mister until the engine warms up(0053). As a pedestrian, do not attempt to cross the road, on the red man, unless you have a driving license(0052). 

 

The Rule of the road, in the UK is, Keep Left, Look Right 1st, in other places, it may be, Keep Right, Look Left 1st (0043).
