The Vehicle Controls(0001).

 

1, STEERING WHEEL(0002).

Turn along the ring wheel to change direction(0003).

2, DIRECTION INDICATOR CONTROL(0004).

Push the control up or down to Indicate whether you intend to turn right or left(0005).

(Can be on either side of the steering wheel)(0006).

3, GEAR LEVER(0007).

Use the gear lever to select and change gears, according to the speed you are travelling(0008).

4, HAND-BRAKE(0009).

Pull the hand-brake up, to keep the vehicle stationary after it has been stopped, it MUST BE used if parked (0010). Use the ratchet release when doing so(0011). It can also be used as an emergency brake(0019).

5, CLUTCH PEDAL(0012).

Press the clutch pedal down, with your left foot, when changing gears(0013). (Automatic vehicles dont have a clutch pedal)(0014).

6, FOOT-BRAKE PEDAL(0015).

Press the foot-brake pedal down, with your right foot, to slow down, or stop the vehicle(0016).

7, ACCELERATOR(0017).

Press the accelerator pedal down, with your right foot, to make the vehicle go faster, or take your foot off, to make it slow down(0018).
