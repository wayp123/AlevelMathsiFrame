The Product Rule_(0001).

 

The Product Rule states if,

 

y=, fx, gx(0002).

 

then_,

d y, dx, = f, dash x, gx, plus f x, g dash x(0003).

b0002

Example(0004).

 

Find the derivative of,

 

y, = x squared, 18x(0005).

 

d y, dx, = 2x, 18x, plus x squared, 18(0006).

 

d y, dx, = 36 x squared, plus 18 x squared(0007).

 

d y, dx,= 54 x squared(0008).

The Quotient Rule_(0009).

 

The Quotient Rule states(0010).

 

If_,

 

y, = f x, over g x(0011).

 

then__,

d y, dx, = f, dash x, gx, minus, f x, g dash x, over g x, squared(0012).

d y, dx, = 10x, minus 5, minus 10x, minus 6, over 2x, minus 1, squared(0016).
