Transformations of Functions(0001).

In Core 3, we finish our table of transformations, with three more reflections(0002). Below, is the complete table, of transformations(0003).

b0002

Reflection _due to polarity_(0004).

y, = negative f x, is a reflection of y, = f x, through the x axis(0005).

y, = f negative x, is a reflection of y, = f x, through the y axis(0006).

y, = mod f x, is a reflection of y, = f x, when_y, is less than 0, through the x-axis(0007).

y, = f mod x, is a reflection of y, = f x, when x is less than 0, through the y-axis(0008).

b0003

y, = f negative 1, x, is a reflection of y, = f x, through the line y=x,y, = f x, must have a, 1 to 1 mapping(0009).

Stretching _due to multiplication_(0010).

y,= f, b x, is stretched towards, the y-axis, if b, is greater than 0, and less than 1 _a fraction_ and stretched away, from the y-axis, if b, is greater than 1(0011). In both cases, the change is by_, 1 over b, units(0012).

y,= A, f x, is stretched towards the x-axis, if A, is greater than 0, and less than 1, and stretched away from the x-axis, if A, is greater than 1(0013). In both cases, the change is by, A, units(0014).

b0004

Translations, or shifting, or sliding _due to addition_(0015).

y, = f, x, minus h, is a translation of f_x_ by h, units, to the right(0016). E g, if h, =5, then f_0_, is reached late, when x = positive 5, so the graph has shifted, to the right of 0, at x = 5(0017).

y, = f, x, plus h, is a translation of f_x_ by h, units, to the left(0018). E g, if h, =5, then f_0_, is reached early, when x = negative 5, so the graph has shifted, to the left of 0, at x = negative 5(0019).

y, = f x, plus k, is a translation of f_x_ by, k units, upwards(0020).

y, = f x, minus k, is a translation of f_x_ by, k units, downwards(0021).

b0005

Example_(0022).

Describe how the function, f x,= x, cubed, can be mapped onto,

f x, = mod, negative 4, mod x, plus 5, by 1 over 3, plus 10(0023).

1, A reflection, through the x-axis, due to the negative, before the 4(0024).

2, A stretch, by 4, in the x-direction(0025).

3, A shift, in the x plane, by 5 units, to the left, alternatively written as a reflection, through x = negative 5(0026).

4, A shift, in the y plane, by 10 units up(0027).

b0006

5, A reflection, through y = x, _inverse function_(0028).

6, A reflection of_, y, = f x, when_ y, is less than 0, through the x-axis(0029).

7, A reflection of__, y, = f x, when__ x, is less than 0, through the y-axis(0030).
