Logarithmic Functions_(0003).

 

In mathematics, you can find the inverse, of an exponential function, by switching x, and y, around(0004).

 

y,= b x, becomes x, = b y_(0005).

The problem arises, on how to find, the value of y(0006). The logarithmic function, solved this problem(0007). All conversions, of logarithmic function, into an exponential function, follow the same pattern(0008).

 

x, = b y, becomes y, = log b, x(0009).

 

The log of a number x, is the power, y, to which the base, b, must be raised, to give the number, x(0010).

Laws of Logarithmic Functions(0015).

 

When X, and Y, are positive(0016).

 

log b, x y, = log b, x, plus log b, y(0017).

 

log b, x over y, = log b, x, minus log b, y(0018).

 

log b, x k, = k, log b, x(0019).
